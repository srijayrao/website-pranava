Our Story

Rooted in tradition,
guided by compassion

Pranava Yoga Studio was born from a simple belief: that ancient wisdom, when held with modern care, can transform lives. Nestled in Hyderabad, we have spent over a decade creating a space where every body is welcomed — regardless of age, ability, or experience.

From our traditional yoga classes to our specialised prenatal program and deeply restorative sound healing sessions, each offering is crafted to meet you exactly where you are on your journey.

— Pranava Studio, Hyderabad
